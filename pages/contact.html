We'd Love to Hear From You

Whether you have a question, need technical support, want to report a bug, suggest a new feature, discuss a collaboration, or simply share your feedback, we're always happy to hear from you.

Every message helps us improve our products and create better experiences for our community.

General Inquiries

For general questions about Basta Community and our products, please contact us by email.

Email: bastacommunity.dev@gmail.com

Technical Support

Need help with one of our applications?

Please include the following information when contacting us:

  • Product name
  • Application version
  • Android version
  • Device model
  • Description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if available)

Providing detailed information helps us resolve issues more efficiently.
