Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

Paperleaf ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Paperleaf collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

By using Paperleaf, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, Paperleaf may collect the following information:

Information You Provide

  • Artwork, sketches, notes, and other content created within the application.
  • Optional information you choose to provide when contacting us.

Automatically Collected Information

If enabled, Paperleaf or its integrated services may collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, language).
  • Application usage statistics.
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information.
  • Anonymous analytics data.

Purchase Information

When you purchase premium features, payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve Paperleaf's features.
  • Save and manage your projects.
  • Maintain application security and stability.
  • Diagnose technical issues and crashes.
  • Improve user experience.
  • Process premium feature access through Google Play.

3. Storage of Your Data

Most project files created in Paperleaf are stored locally on your device.

If cloud synchronization or backup features become available, your data may also be securely stored on trusted cloud service providers after obtaining the necessary permissions.

4. Third-Party Services

Paperleaf may use trusted third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Cloud Storage (if applicable)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when:

  • Required by law.
  • Necessary to provide application functionality through trusted service providers.
  • Needed to protect the security, rights, or safety of users and our services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Paperleaf is not specifically directed toward children under the age required by applicable law.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your local laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with the updated Effective Date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: bastacommunity.dev@gmail.com

Instagram: @Paperleaf.App
