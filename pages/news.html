Paperleaf Progress

Paperleaf is currently under active development. Recent improvements include:

  • Improved sketchbook management.
  • Better page organization.
  • Enhanced page-flip animation.
  • Performance optimizations.
  • User interface refinements.
  • Preparation for future premium features.

More exciting features are currently in development.

Planning

Paperbook

Development planning has officially begun for Paperbook.

Paperbook is designed to become a complete platform where creators can publish, sell, and share interactive flip books while readers enjoy immersive reading experiences.

Future capabilities will include: Creator profiles, Digital bookstore, Personal libraries, Book publishing, Secure purchases, and Reading progress synchronization.

In Development

PageFlip Library

Our proprietary PageFlip Library continues to evolve. Current development focuses on: Smoother animations, Improved rendering performance, Better memory efficiency, and Easier integration for Android applications.

This library serves as the foundation for the reading experience across our ecosystem.
