Featured: Paperleaf

Create. Draw. Write. Imagine.

Paperleaf is a digital flipbook and creative notebook for Android that brings together drawing, writing, planning, and visual storytelling in one elegant workspace.

Designed for artists, students, designers, writers, and creative thinkers, Paperleaf recreates the natural feeling of working with real paper while offering the flexibility of digital tools.

Key Features

  • Digital sketchbooks
  • Multiple paper, book and textures
  • Available themes, cover and stickers
  • Drawing and handwriting tools
  • Image import
  • Realistic page-flip experience
  • PDF export
  • PLF export
  • Cloud synchronization
  • Premium creative tools
In Development

PageFlip Library

The Engine Behind Every Page Turn

PageFlip Library is our proprietary Android library that delivers realistic and smooth page-turn animations. It powers the reading experience across our applications, making digital books feel natural and enjoyable.

Features

  • Realistic page curl animation
  • High-performance rendering
  • Lightweight architecture
  • Easy Android integration
  • Highly customizable
  • Optimized for books, journals, notebooks, and sketchbooks
Future

Paperbook

Create, Publish, Sell, and Read Digital Flip Books

Paperbook is our upcoming digital publishing platform that allows creators to transform their work into beautiful interactive flip books.

Whether you create children's stories, comics, illustrated books, educational materials, novels, magazines, or personal journals, Paperbook will provide an easy way to publish and share your creations.

Readers will also enjoy a dedicated reading experience with realistic page-turn animations powered by our PageFlip Library.

Planned Features

  • Create digital flip books
  • Import projects from Paperleaf
  • Interactive page-turn reading
  • Publish books online
  • Personal digital library
  • Buy and sell books
  • Creator profiles
  • Secure digital purchases
  • Cloud synchronization
  • Reading progress synchronization
  • Offline reading
  • Bookmark pages
  • Reading statistics
