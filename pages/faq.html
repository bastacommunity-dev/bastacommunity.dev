Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

General

What is Basta Community?

Basta Community is an independent software developer focused on creating applications that support creativity, digital art, storytelling, and publishing. Our products are designed to be simple, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone.

What products are developed by Basta Community?

Our current ecosystem includes:

  • Paperleaf – A digital sketchbook and creative notebook.
  • PageFlip Library – A realistic page-turn animation engine for Android.
  • Paperbook (Coming Soon) – A platform for creating, publishing, selling, and reading digital flip books.

Are your applications free?

Most applications provide free features that anyone can use. Some advanced tools and services may require a premium purchase through Google Play.

Paperleaf

What is Paperleaf?

Paperleaf is a digital sketchbook designed for drawing, handwriting, note-taking, journaling, and visual storytelling with a natural paper-like experience.

Where are my projects stored?

By default, your projects are stored locally on your device. Future versions may offer optional cloud backup and synchronization.

Does Paperleaf require an internet connection?

No. Most creative features work completely offline. An internet connection is only required for services such as updates, cloud features, or online purchases.

Will Paperleaf support cloud synchronization?

Yes. Cloud synchronization is planned for a future release to help users safely back up and restore their projects across devices.

Can I export my work?

Yes. Paperleaf is planned to support exporting projects in formats such as PDF and images, making it easier to print or share your creations.

Paperbook

What is Paperbook?

Paperbook is an upcoming digital publishing platform where creators can transform their work into interactive flip books. Users will be able to publish, sell, purchase, and read digital books in an immersive reading experience.

What types of books will Paperbook support?

Paperbook is planned to support: Children's books, Comics, Illustrated stories, Novels, Educational books, Magazines, Portfolios, Journals, and Art books.

Will creators be able to sell their books?

Yes. Paperbook is being designed to provide creators with opportunities to publish and sell their digital books through the platform.

Purchases

How do I restore Premium features?

Premium purchases made through Google Play can be restored using the same Google account originally used for the purchase.

Can I request a refund?

Refund requests are handled according to Google Play's refund policies. Please refer to Google Play Support for more information.

Privacy & Security

Does Basta Community collect my personal information?

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Is my artwork safe?

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. Projects stored locally remain under your control, while future cloud services will use secure storage technologies.

Support

How can I report a bug?

Please visit our Contact page and include: Product name, App version, Android version, Device model, Description of the issue, and Screenshots (if available).

How can I suggest a new feature?

We welcome ideas from our community. Feature requests can be submitted through our Contact page or official social media channels.

Future Development

Will more applications be released?

Yes. Basta Community is continuously developing new creative tools and expanding its ecosystem to support artists, writers, educators, and creators around the world.
